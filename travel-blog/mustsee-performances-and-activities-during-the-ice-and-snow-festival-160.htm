Must-See Performances and Activities During the Ice and Snow Festival

  • 首页

  • Travel Blog

The Ice and Snow Festival is one of the most highly anticipated events in many northern cities of China, especially in Harbin. Held annually from January to February, this festival showcases an incredible array of winter activities, performances, and spectacular ice sculptures. With its origins in Harbin, the festival has grown to attract tourists from all over the world who come to witness the magnificent ice and snow art, as well as enjoy a variety of entertainment and cultural performances. Whether you’re an adventure enthusiast or simply looking to enjoy the beauty of winter, there are plenty of performances and activities that should be on your must-see list during the Ice and Snow Festival.

1. Harbin Ice Sculpture Festival

The Harbin Ice Sculpture Festival is undoubtedly the highlight of the Ice and Snow Festival. It’s famous for its grand ice sculptures, some of which are massive, intricate, and illuminated by multicolored lights at night, creating a surreal atmosphere. Visitors can expect to see everything from life-sized replicas of famous landmarks to fantastical creatures and imaginative structures.

Key Highlights of the Ice Sculpture Festival:

  • Ice and Snow World: This is one of the most iconic parts of the festival. Located on the banks of the Songhua River, it features large-scale sculptures carved from blocks of ice, which are collected from the river. These sculptures are often illuminated at night, creating a breathtaking and magical scene.
  • Ice Sculpture Competition: Skilled ice sculptors from around the world gather to showcase their talents in a live competition, where they create intricate, detailed ice artworks. This event offers a unique opportunity to witness the mastery of ice carving in real-time.
  • Ice Swimming and Diving: For those who are brave enough to experience the cold in an extraordinary way, the festival offers opportunities to watch or even participate in ice swimming and ice diving. These activities are popular among the local people and tourists seeking a unique winter thrill.

2. Snow Sculpture Art Expo

While the ice sculptures steal the show, the Snow Sculpture Art Expo is also an important part of the Ice and Snow Festival. The expo takes place in Harbin's Sun Island Scenic Area and is home to gigantic snow sculptures created by talented artists from around the world. The sheer size and intricacy of the snow sculptures are awe-inspiring, with themes ranging from traditional Chinese mythology to modern pop culture references.

What to Expect at the Snow Sculpture Art Expo:

  • Massive Snow Sculptures: The sculptures at Sun Island are much larger than the ones made from ice, with some towering over 10 meters (33 feet) tall. These large snow sculptures are intricately carved and often depict scenes from Chinese history, legends, and folklore.
  • International Snow Sculpture Competition: This competition brings together snow sculptors from all corners of the world, creating a melting pot of diverse artistic styles. It’s a great opportunity to see how different cultures approach snow art and gain insight into the global nature of this beautiful art form.
  • Interactive Snow Sculptures: Some snow sculptures are interactive, allowing visitors to climb on them, take photos, and engage with the sculptures in a playful way. This makes it a fun activity for families and children.

3. Ice Sailing and Snowmobiles

For those who enjoy a bit of adventure, the Ice and Snow Festival offers exciting outdoor activities, such as ice sailing and snowmobiles. These activities give visitors the chance to experience the winter landscape from a different perspective and add some thrill to their visit.

Ice Sailing:

  • Ice sailing, or "ice yachting," is an exhilarating sport where visitors ride a sailboat equipped with skis instead of wheels. This activity takes place on frozen lakes or rivers, where the strong winter winds propel the boat across the icy surface at high speeds.
  • Harbin's Songhua River is one of the most popular places to try ice sailing. It’s a thrilling way to explore the icy terrain while enjoying the stunning winter scenery.

Snowmobiles:

  • Snowmobiling is another popular activity that allows visitors to zoom through the snowy landscapes of Harbin. Snowmobile tours take you through the snowy forests, frozen lakes, and valleys, providing a fun and exciting way to experience the winter wonderland.

4. Ice Skating and Ice Sculpting Shows

Ice skating is a traditional winter activity, and during the Ice and Snow Festival, visitors can watch spectacular ice skating performances at various ice rinks, such as the Harbin Ice Skating Rink and the Ice and Snow World. These performances feature talented skaters performing elaborate routines, often set to music, and are a great way to enjoy the winter vibe of the festival.

Ice Sculpting Shows:

  • Throughout the festival, you can watch live ice sculpting shows where artists work their magic, transforming massive blocks of ice into intricate sculptures right before your eyes. These performances are both mesmerizing and impressive, showcasing the artistry and skill of the ice sculptors.
  • The ice sculpting shows are often accompanied by music and light displays, adding an extra layer of excitement to the event.

5. Ice Fishing and Fishing Festivals

For those who enjoy more leisurely activities, ice fishing is another traditional winter sport that takes place during the Ice and Snow Festival. The frozen lakes and rivers around Harbin are great spots for ice fishing, and visitors can participate by cutting holes in the thick ice and casting their lines into the water below.

Ice Fishing:

  • Ice fishing is a serene yet thrilling activity where you wait for fish to bite in the frozen waters beneath you. This activity can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels, making it a great family-friendly activity.
  • Harbin’s Songhua River is a popular location for ice fishing during the festival, and the fish caught here are often used in local dishes and served at nearby restaurants.

Fishing Festivals:

  • During the Ice and Snow Festival, you can also experience local fishing festivals where people compete to catch the largest or most unusual fish. These festivals are a great way to experience local culture and enjoy the winter atmosphere in a relaxed setting.

6. Winter Cultural Performances

In addition to the outdoor activities and ice sculptures, the Ice and Snow Festival is also an excellent opportunity to experience the rich cultural heritage of Harbin and the surrounding areas. Various traditional performances take place during the festival, offering a glimpse into the region’s unique customs and traditions.

Traditional Chinese Folk Dance and Music:

  • One of the highlights of the festival is the traditional Chinese folk performances, which include vibrant dances and musical performances. These performances often feature local artists and cultural groups, and they are an opportunity to experience the region’s ethnic diversity and folk traditions.
  • The Harbin Grand Theatre is a great venue for cultural performances, offering a variety of shows that celebrate both local and national art forms. These performances can range from traditional Chinese opera to modern dance and musical theater, providing a rich cultural experience.

Ice Music Concerts:

  • For a truly unique experience, some performances during the Ice and Snow Festival take place in specially constructed ice venues, where musicians play instruments made from ice. These ice music concerts offer an otherworldly experience, as the instruments produce eerie, ethereal sounds against the backdrop of the icy surroundings.

7. Winter Sports Competitions

The Ice and Snow Festival also includes several winter sports competitions where athletes from around the world compete in ice and snow sports. Some of these events are held on the frozen lakes and snow-covered fields around Harbin, while others take place at specially designed winter sports venues.

Ice Hockey:

  • Ice hockey is a popular sport during the Ice and Snow Festival, and visitors can watch thrilling ice hockey matches featuring local teams and international players. The matches are fast-paced and exciting, making them a great event for sports enthusiasts.

Snowboard and Skiing Competitions:

  • Skiing and snowboarding competitions are held at various locations near Harbin, where professional and amateur athletes compete in slalom, downhill racing, and freestyle events. Watching these competitions can be exciting for fans of winter sports.

8. Local Food Tasting Events

No visit to Harbin’s Ice and Snow Festival is complete without sampling the city’s delicious winter cuisine. The festival often features food markets and tasting events where you can try local delicacies that are popular in the region.

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Hotpot: This hearty dish is perfect for warming up during the chilly winter months. Harbin's version of hotpot features a variety of meats, vegetables, and spices that are cooked in a steaming pot of broth.
  • Ice Cream: Despite the cold weather, Harbin’s ice cream, made with locally sourced ingredients, is a must-try. The thick, creamy ice cream comes in unique flavors such as rose and milk tea.
  • Kebabs: Grilled lamb or beef kebabs are popular street food in Harbin, and they are often served with a spicy dipping sauce.

The Ice and Snow Festival in Harbin is a celebration of winter’s beauty, culture, and the excitement of outdoor activities. From breathtaking ice and snow sculptures to thrilling outdoor adventures, there is no shortage of things to see and do. Whether you're an art enthusiast, an adrenaline junkie, or someone looking to experience something new, the festival offers something for everyone. Be sure to plan your visit around these must-see performances and activities for a truly unforgettable experience.
