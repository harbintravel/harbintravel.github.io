How to Travel from Harbin to Mohe and How Long Does It Take?

  • 首页

  • Travel Blog

Mohe, located in China’s northernmost region, is a captivating destination known for its stunning natural scenery, vibrant auroras, and unique subarctic culture. If you're planning a journey from Harbin to Mohe, here’s a comprehensive guide covering transportation options, travel time, and tips for a seamless trip.

Distance Between Harbin and Mohe

The distance between Harbin and Mohe is approximately 1,200 kilometers (746 miles). Depending on your mode of transport, the journey can take anywhere from several hours to more than a day.

Travel Options from Harbin to Mohe

By Train

Regular Trains

Regular trains from Harbin to Mohe are an economical and popular option. These trains are equipped with hard seats, soft seats, and sleeper options for overnight travel.

  • Travel Time: The journey typically takes 16–18 hours, depending on the specific train schedule.
  • Ticket Prices:
    • Hard Seat: Around 150 RMB
    • Hard Sleeper: Approximately 270–300 RMB
    • Soft Sleeper: Around 400 RMB
  • Experience: Regular trains offer a budget-friendly way to experience China’s vast countryside. However, they may not provide the level of comfort some travelers prefer for such a long trip.

High-Speed Trains

Currently, there are no direct high-speed trains from Harbin to Mohe. If high-speed rail becomes available in the future, it could significantly reduce travel time.

By Flight

Flights are the fastest way to travel between Harbin and Mohe. Harbin Taiping International Airport connects to Mohe Gulian Airport with daily flights offered by various airlines.

  • Travel Time: Approximately 2 hours.
  • Ticket Prices: Generally range from 700–1,500 RMB, depending on the season and booking time.
  • Experience: Flights are the most convenient option for travelers with limited time, offering scenic aerial views of the snow-covered landscapes.

Tips for Flying:

  • Book tickets in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.
  • Mohe Gulian Airport is small, and transportation to the city center is available via taxis or shuttle buses.

By Bus

Long-distance buses from Harbin to Mohe are another option, although less commonly chosen due to the long travel time and potential discomfort.

  • Travel Time: Around 20–24 hours.
  • Ticket Prices: Approximately 250–350 RMB.
  • Experience: This option is suitable for budget-conscious travelers but is less comfortable for such a long journey.

By Car

Driving from Harbin to Mohe is an adventurous way to experience northern China’s vast landscapes.

  • Travel Time: Approximately 15–18 hours, depending on weather and road conditions.
  • Route: The most common route is via the G10 Suifenhe-Manzhouli Expressway and S209 Provincial Road.
  • Experience: A self-drive trip offers flexibility to stop at scenic points and small towns along the way.

Tips for Driving:

  • Ensure your vehicle is equipped for cold weather conditions, especially in winter.
  • Check road conditions and plan your route carefully, as snow and ice can impact travel.
  • Carry essential supplies, including food, water, and a first aid kit, for emergencies.

Best Time to Travel from Harbin to Mohe

Winter (December to February)

Winter is the peak season for visiting Mohe. The town transforms into a winter wonderland with snow-covered landscapes, ice sculptures, and the possibility of seeing the northern lights.

  • Pros: Ideal for experiencing Mohe’s famous winter activities.
  • Cons: Extremely cold temperatures and high demand for transportation and accommodations.

Summer (June to August)

Mohe’s summer offers lush greenery and mild temperatures, making it perfect for exploring the great outdoors.

  • Pros: Comfortable weather for hiking and sightseeing.
  • Cons: Fewer winter-specific attractions.

Tips for Traveling from Harbin to Mohe

Pack for the Weather

  • Winter: Bring heavy winter clothing, thermal layers, gloves, hats, and waterproof boots.
  • Summer: Pack lightweight clothing, but also include a jacket for cooler evenings.

Plan Accommodation in Advance

Mohe has limited accommodations, especially during peak seasons. Popular options include hotels, guesthouses, and traditional wooden lodges. Booking early ensures better choices.

Explore Mohe’s Attractions

Once you arrive in Mohe, there are plenty of attractions to explore:

  • Arctic Village (Beijicun): China’s northernmost settlement, offering unique cultural experiences.
  • Santa Claus House: A charming attraction with festive decorations year-round.
  • Northern Lights Viewing: In winter, Mohe is one of the best places in China to see auroras.

Stay Safe

  • If traveling in winter, be prepared for icy roads and cold weather.
  • Keep important documents, cash, and travel essentials easily accessible.

Local Cuisine

Don’t miss the chance to try Mohe’s local specialties, such as wild mushroom soup, river fish, and hearty stews that are perfect for cold weather.

Summary

Traveling from Harbin to Mohe offers various options, each catering to different budgets and preferences. Whether you choose the comfort of a flight, the scenic charm of a train ride, or the flexibility of driving, your journey will be rewarded with the unique beauty and culture of Mohe. With careful planning and preparation, this trip is sure to be an unforgettable adventure.
